Faculty members of the College of Pharmacy, Sookmyung Women’s University, including our lab, were selected for the Ministry of Education–supported **Glocal Lab Program** with up to nine years of funding support. Through this collaborative initiative, our lab will contribute to research on next-generation therapeutics for healthy aging and regenerative medicine.
